Privacy Policy

1. General Principles

Fine Defense Co., Ltd. complies with the Personal Information Protection provisions of the “Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection” and all relevant regulations. The company is committed to protecting the personal information of its users.

This policy may be revised in accordance with applicable laws or company guidelines. Any changes will be announced through the company’s website.

2. Items Collected and Purpose of Use

  • Name, user ID, password, email address, phone number
  • Address, payment information (bank or credit card details)
  • Cookies (ID) – for providing customized services

Users may refuse the use of cookies; however, certain services may be restricted if cookie storage is disabled.

3. Retention Period of Personal Information

  • Records of contracts or withdrawal of orders: 5 years
  • Records of payment and supply of goods: 5 years
  • Records of consumer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

As a rule, personal information is not provided to third parties without the user’s consent. Exceptions may apply when required by law or by an authorized government agency.

5. Access and Correction of Personal Information

Users may access and correct their personal information at any time. The responsibility for managing ID and password information lies with the user.

6. Withdrawal of Membership

Users may request membership withdrawal at any time through the account settings menu on the website.

7. Technical Safeguards for Personal Information

  • Encrypted storage of passwords and personal data
  • Operation of anti-virus software and intrusion prevention systems
  • Regular security inspections and monitoring
