Privacy Policy

1. General Provisions

FINE DEFENSE INC. complies with the relevant laws and guidelines of the Republic of Korea regarding the protection of personal information and is committed to safeguarding users’ personal data.

This policy may be amended in accordance with applicable laws or company regulations. Any changes will be announced through the company website.

2. Collection and Use of Personal Information

  • Name, ID, password, email address, and phone number
  • Address and payment information (bank or credit card details)
  • Cookies (for customized service and user convenience)

Users may choose to refuse cookie storage through browser settings. However, certain services may be limited as a result.

3. Retention Period of Personal Information

  • Records of contracts or subscription withdrawals: 5 years
  • Records of payment and supply of goods: 5 years
  • Records of customer complaints or dispute resolution: 3 years

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The company does not provide personal information to third parties without the user’s consent, except when required by law.

5. Access and Correction of Personal Information

Users may access or correct their personal information at any time. The user is responsible for managing their own ID and password.

6. Membership Withdrawal

Users may withdraw their membership at any time through the account management section of the website.

7. Technical Measures for the Protection of Personal Information

  • Passwords and personal data are stored in encrypted form.
  • Antivirus programs and intrusion prevention systems are operated.
  • Regular security inspections and monitoring are conducted.
