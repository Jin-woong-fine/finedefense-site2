CEO Message

Welcome, and thank you for visiting the Fine Defense website.

Fine Defense Co., Ltd. is a specialized engineering company dedicated to the design and manufacturing of fixed and towed underwater cables for sonar, magnetic, and electric field systems, as well as marine exploration cables tailored to diverse operational requirements.

Through our expertise in subsea-environment design, advanced materials, mass-production processes, and qualification technologies, we have successfully completed national R&D programs and localized key components previously reliant on imports—enabling entry into the military supply chain. In the commercial sector, we continue to expand our presence through high-quality products and customer-focused performance.

Leveraging accumulated experience and proven capabilities, we remain committed to delivering customized solutions that support the mission success of our partners and promote shared growth.

We sincerely appreciate your continued interest and support.

Thank you.

Fine Defense Co., Ltd.
