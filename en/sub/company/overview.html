About Us

Connecting Maritime Defense Systems
with Underwater Cable & Connector Technologies

FINE DEFENSE is a specialized engineering company in the maritime defense industry, dedicated to the design, manufacturing, and testing of sonar systems and defense-grade submarine and underwater cables, including towed and fixed underwater cable systems, as well as marine exploration cables.

As a technology-driven small and strong enterprise composed of a highly skilled team of engineers, FINE DEFENSE independently performs the entire process from system design and manufacturing to testing and evaluation. The company has accumulated core capabilities in subsea-environment-optimized design, materials engineering, mass-production manufacturing, and qualification evaluation.

Based on these technological strengths, FINE DEFENSE has completed multiple national R&D programs and successfully localized imported defense products, enabling entry into the military market. In parallel, the company continues to expand its presence in the commercial sector by delivering high-quality products and maintaining a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

With proven technologies and rigorous quality management, FINE DEFENSE provides customized engineering solutions tailored to mission requirements, continuing to grow as a reliable and trusted partner in the global maritime defense industry.
