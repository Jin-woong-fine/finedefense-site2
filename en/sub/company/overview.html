Company Overview

Connecting the ocean through technology — Fine Defense leads the way.

Fine Defense Co., Ltd. is a specialized manufacturer dedicated to the design, production, and testing of defense-grade underwater cables, including fixed and towed array sonar cables for naval platforms such as submarines and surface vessels.

As a technology-driven company composed of experienced engineers, we independently manage the entire process—from design and manufacturing to qualification testing. With accumulated technical expertise and rigorous quality management, Fine Defense delivers highly reliable solutions for both domestic and international maritime defense programs.

Based on proven capabilities and a strong commitment to quality, Fine Defense continues to grow as a trusted partner in the global naval defense industry.
