Privacy Policy

General Provisions

FINE DEFENSE Co., Ltd. (“the Company”) complies with the Personal Information Protection Act and relevant guidelines issued by the Ministry of Science and ICT. The Company establishes and complies with this Privacy Policy to protect users’ personal information and ensure transparency.

This policy may be amended in accordance with changes in laws, government regulations, or internal company policy. When revisions are made, updates will be announced on the Company website at least seven (7) days prior to implementation.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

  • Collected Items: Name, email address, contact number, affiliation (optional), inquiry details
  • Collection Method: Inquiry forms submitted through the website or via email communication

Purpose of Collection and Use

  • To respond to customer inquiries and provide technical support
  • To fulfill contractual obligations and provide requested services
  • To comply with legal obligations or government requests

Retention and Use Period

Personal information will be retained only for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes of collection and will be deleted once those purposes are fulfilled, unless retention is required by law.

  • Inquiry records: Up to 1 year after inquiry completion
  • Contract and transaction records: Up to 5 years in accordance with consumer protection laws

Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Company does not disclose or provide users’ personal information to third parties without consent, except as required by applicable laws or governmental authorities.

Entrustment of Personal Information Processing

Personal information may be entrusted to external service providers only when necessary to provide services, and the Company ensures compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

Destruction of Personal Information

Once the retention period expires or the processing purpose has been achieved, personal information will be destroyed without delay using the following methods:

  • Electronic files: Permanently deleted using non-recoverable methods
  • Printed materials: Shredded or incinerated

Technical and Administrative Safeguards

  • Encryption and secure storage of personal data
  • Access control and network protection systems
  • Regular security software updates and antivirus measures
  • Employee training on data protection responsibilities

Data Subject Rights

Users may request access to, correction, or deletion of their personal information at any time. The Company will promptly respond in accordance with applicable laws.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be modified to reflect changes in legal or operational requirements. Updates will be posted on the Company website.
