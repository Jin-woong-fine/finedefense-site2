Calculation Method

The braid geometry is calculated based on standard helical braid theory using braid pitch and carrier configuration.

Braid angle:
α = arctan [ (2π · (Dc + 2d) · P) / C ]

Coverage:
K = 100 · (2F − F²)
F = (E · P · d) / sin α

Estimated outer diameter after braiding:
Dout = Dc + 2 · d · cos α

where d is the wire diameter, Dc is the core diameter, P is the braid pitch, C is the number of carriers, and E is the number of ends per carrier.
