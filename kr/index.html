Manufacturing & Testing Capabilities

From stranding and armoring to extrusion and reliability testing, FINE DEFENSE operates complete in-house facilities to ensure the reliability and quality of every subsea cable we manufacture.

Stranding Machine

Core wire stranding system ensuring uniform tension and precision alignment for multi-conductor subsea cables.

Steel Wire Armoring Line

Reinforcing cable structure with precision steel wire armoring for superior mechanical strength and durability.

Extrusion Line

Advanced extrusion equipment for consistent insulation and sheathing across all cable types.

Mechanical & Environmental Test Facility

Integrated systems for hydrostatic, tensile, bending, vibration, and temperature testing to ensure reliability under real-world conditions.

Electrical & Optical Test Equipment

Portable instruments for continuity, insulation resistance, and optical loss verification in subsea cable systems.
